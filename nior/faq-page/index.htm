How to get glowing skin?

Glowing skin is a sign of healthy skin. Consider these skincare tips to help your skin glow.
1. Wash your face regularly with a gentle facial cleanser
2. Exfoliate a couple of times a week at most
3. Apply a facial toner
4. Use a vitamin C serum for brighter skin
5. Apply moisturizer

How often should I exfoliate?

Most experts advise that you exfoliate two to three times per week — as long as your skin can handle
it. Chemical exfoliants tend to be fine to use more regularly. Physical methods, on the other hand, maybe
too abrasive to use multiple times a week.

Can I use the same product both day & night?

Of course! You can use the same set of skincare in the morning and evening if it suits you and that’s
what you prefer to do. Using a different set can be expensive, although it might eventually work out to be
the same, as each product lasts longer. But ultimately, there’s no need to use different skincare for day and
night routines.

How do I know if my skin is dry/oily/dehydrated?

If your skin appears shiny throughout, you most likely have oily skin. If it feels tight and is flaky or
scaly, you likely have dry skin. If the shine is only in your T-zone, you may have combination skin. If your
skin feels hydrated and comfortable, but not oily, you likely have normal skin.

How to ensure proper skin care with Nior products?

Think of your skin-care routine as consisting of three main steps.
● Cleansing — Washing your face
● Toning — Balancing the skin
● Moisturizing — Hydrating and softening the skin

BEAUTY (COLOR COSMETICS)

What is the best mascara for me?

Choosing one mascara from the hundreds of options on the market is a daunting task, but it really
comes down to your personal needs. On hot and humid days, you may opt for a long-lasting waterproof
mascara that won’t sweat off. For an easy falsies look, a volumizing mascara will do the trick. If your goal
is fluttery long lashes, lengthening mascaras will be your go-to.

What Eyeliner pencil best suits me?

Liquid liner - can go on perfectly but is almost too thin or too thick for its own good. A thin liner can make
the eye look smaller. A thick liner can make it look overdramatic for doing ordinary outings like grocery
shopping.
Pencil liner - you’ll have to pull your eyelid to drag it across. Some pencils go on smoothly and cheap ones
don’t always go on smoothly. These are good backups and you’ll be tempted to line your lips and pencil in
your eyebrows with them. You’ll always have it if the others run out.

How do I choose a lipstick?

Matte lipsticks are generally considered classier and subtler, which is great if you’re going for a more
subdued or formal look. Glossy lipstick is considered more dramatic and playful, and the sheen will call
more attention to your lips if that’s a feature you want to play up.
